## Do's
- ITC Garamond Condensed for ALL display headings (72, 102, 120px), -0.03em
- Grove Green text on Buttermilk/Farmhouse Gray bg (AAA contrast)
- 20px radius on large images (signature)
- **Zest Yellow ONLY for primary CTA bg** (with Grove Green text)
- 8-10px radius minimum on interactive
- GT Alpina Typewriter for body 16px weight 400
- Alternating bg colors in major sections

## Don'ts
- No pure black or stark white (Grove Green + Buttermilk)
- No generic sans-serif for headings (Garamond Condensed critical)
- No sharp 0px corners on interactive (8px minimum)
- No Zest Yellow for small text or secondary
- No excessive shadows or gradients
- No breaking visual rhythm of alternating bg colors
- No dense info packing (spacious required)
