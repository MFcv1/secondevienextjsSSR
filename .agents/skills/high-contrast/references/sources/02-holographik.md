Source: https://styles.refero.design/style/f46616d6-5138-408b-a29d-b6749a6f3b04  
Site: https://www.holographik.co  
North star: High-contrast monochrome blueprint  
Theme: dark

## Extracted Palette

| Name | Hex | Role |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Absolute Black | `#000000` | Page backgrounds, primary text, foundational borders, hero background |
| Pure White | `#ffffff` | Borders, dividers, card edges, inverse surfaces |
| Ash Gray | `#aaaaaa` | Subtle panels and lower-prominence content zones |

## Typography

- Primary family: Helvetica Neue.
- Fallback: Arial.
- Weight: 400 only.
- Sizes observed: 16px, 26px.
- Line heights: 1.00, 1.25.
- Letter spacing: -0.0200em.

## Layout Intelligence

- Immersive black canvas with large layered graphic elements.
- Visual identity relies on monochrome tension, not chromatic variation.
- Vertical spacing can be extremely large: 200px section gaps.
- The system is block-based, direct, and blueprint-like.
- Component language contrasts strict typography with extreme pill geometry.

## Components

| Component | Role | Treatment |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Navigation Link | Top and footer links | Pure white or absolute black text, no underline by default |
| Accent Bordered Element | Button-like interactive element | White/black inverse surface with 1px border and 160px radius |
| Text Block | Descriptive content | Helvetica Neue 16px, tight line-height, high contrast pairings |
| Hero Headline | Primary statement | 26px, weight 400, tight line-height, negative tracking |

## Rules Worth Carrying Forward

- Monochrome can still feel highly branded through radius, spacing, and type discipline.
- A 160px radius can become a signature when used intentionally.
- Do not introduce extra colors into a strict black-white-gray system.
- Use huge section gaps to create an architectural feeling.
- Keep all typography one weight if the brand depends on industrial consistency.
