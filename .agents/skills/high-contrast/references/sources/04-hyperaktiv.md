## Typography

- Headings: Studiofeixensans.
- Heading fallbacks: Arial Black, Impact.
- Body: DM Sans.
- Body fallbacks: Inter, Lato.
- Heading weights: 400, 700.
- Body weight: 400.
- Sizes observed: 14px through 90px.
- Line heights range from 0.89 to 2.00.

## Layout Intelligence

- Light high-contrast system based on white field, black typography, and one electric blue.
- Hero can use full-width imagery with centered direct typography.
- Cards are text-only and often borderless.
- Inputs are square, functional, and plain.
- One exception to square geometry: pill/floating action buttons around 20px radius.

## Components

| Component | Role | Treatment |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Primary Filled Button | Main CTA | Action Blue fill, white text, 0px radius |
| Outline Button | Secondary action | White fill, black text, black 1px border |
| Pill Button | Specific action | White fill, black border, 20px radius |
| Input Field | Form entry | White fill, gray border, square corners |
| Feature Card | Text section | Transparent, no border, no shadow, layout-defined separation |

## Rules Worth Carrying Forward

- High contrast does not need to be dark; white, black, and blue can be equally sharp.
- Use one chromatic action color with discipline.
- Keep form controls crisp and highly legible.
- Use strong display type for impact, but rely on a readable body font for comprehension.
- Let empty space and alignment replace decorative borders.
