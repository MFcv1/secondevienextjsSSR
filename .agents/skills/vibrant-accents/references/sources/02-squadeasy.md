# SquadEasy - Vibrant Accents Source Notes

## Core read

SquadEasy is the playful block-color archetype. It uses large, flat, saturated
surfaces, bold typography, angled photo cutouts, and pill actions. The page is
not subtle: color is allowed to occupy large sections, but it remains organized
through strong black/white contrast and a limited set of surface roles.

This source is useful when vibrant accents should feel energetic, human, and
campaign-like rather than refined fintech/product minimal.

## Typography

- Body family or Inter fallback.
- Weights: 400, 500, 700.
- Headlines use a heavy "Black" style at huge sizes.
- Display sizes range from 50px to 220px.
- Tight line heights: around 0.87 to 1 for main display.
- Body/links use tight negative tracking.
- The headline should feel blocky, direct, and loud.

## Structure

- Base unit: 4px.
- Density: comfortable.
- Section gap: 100px.
- Card padding: 16px.
- Element gap: 16px.
- Buttons: 100px radius.
- Nav elements: 14px radius.
- Some cards: 0px radius, especially large color-block info cards.

## Component lessons

1. Electric Lime pill CTA
   - Fill #e4ff60.
   - Black text.
   - 100px radius.
   - Most persuasive action style.

2. Black pill CTA
   - Fill black.
   - White text.
   - 100px radius.
   - Useful on lighter color fields.

3. Deep Violet squared card
   - Large flat color block.
   - No shadow.
   - Often 0px radius.
   - Generous bottom padding for visual weight.

4. Angled photo cutouts
   - Images break the box.
   - Crops feel dynamic and human.
   - They should not be placed in simple card frames.

5. Hot Pink hover/highlight
   - Use for link hover, highlight text, or border detail.
   - Not the main CTA when Electric Lime is present.

## Design lessons

- Vibrance can occupy big surfaces if the palette is finite.
- Black text is the anchor that keeps color blocks readable.
- Buttons can be extremely soft while cards stay square.
- Photo cutouts add human energy that flat colors cannot provide alone.
- Do not apologize for the color; make it structural.

## Anti-patterns

- Do not use generic gray for interaction.
- Do not add subtle shadows or gradients to flat color blocks.
- Do not make headlines delicate.
- Do not put all images in rectangular boxes.
- Do not introduce extra major section colors.
- Do not use square corners on buttons.
