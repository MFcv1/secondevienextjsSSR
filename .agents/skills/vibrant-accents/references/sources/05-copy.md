# Copy - Vibrant Accents Source Notes

## Core read

Copy is the enterprise violet archetype: high-tech B2B polish, muted light
surfaces, precise typography, and a vivid violet accent that drives CTAs,
active states, and process highlights. It is more corporate and controlled than
SquadEasy, less gradient-forward than Stripe, and sharper than Base44.

This is useful when vibrant accents need to feel authoritative in enterprise
or strategic SaaS contexts.

## Typography

- Display: ABC Normal or Montserrat fallback.
- Display weights: 500 and 600.
- Display sizes: 24-88px.
- Large display tracking: -0.02em at 56-88px.
- Functional text: Inter, 12-24px.
- Inter weights: 400-700.
- Functional text line-heights vary from 1.17 to 2.
- Major headlines should not use Inter; Inter is for function.

## Structure

- Base unit: 4px.
- Density: comfortable.
- Section gap: 72px.
- Card padding: 30px.
- Element gap: 16px.
- General radius: 4px.
- Buttons: 4px.
- Inputs may use 0px radius in data-entry contexts.
- No shadow for depth; use surface variation and precise spacing.

## Component lessons

1. Violet primary CTA
   - Fill #693edf.
   - White or high-contrast text.
   - 4px radius.
   - Primary action and active state.

2. Login/secondary outline
   - Midnight Ink text.
   - Ash Veil stroke.
   - 4px radius.

3. Cloud Burst input
   - Light blue-gray fill.
   - Slate Echo border.
   - Can visually blend into canvas.

4. Process step
   - Transparent base.
   - Violet shades highlight active progression.
   - Good for sequential enterprise explanations.

5. Tag badge
   - Transparent.
   - No radius or padding.
   - Categorization stays typographic.

## Design lessons

- Enterprise vibrant accents should be precise, not playful.
- Violet can feel strategic if paired with gray-blue surfaces.
- Do not use shadows when surface layers and spacing are enough.
- Custom display type makes B2B pages feel proprietary.
- Accent should drive process and action, not random decoration.

## Anti-patterns

- Do not use violet for body text.
- Do not introduce additional radii.
- Do not use shadows for elevation.
- Do not use Inter for major headlines.
- Do not add extra saturated colors.
- Do not make process steps visually noisy.
