# UNVEIL® — Style Reference

## Tokens — Colors (BINARY!)
| Name | Value | Token | Role |
|------|-------|-------|------|
| Pitch Black | `#000000` | `--color-pitch-black` | All text, button borders, nav |
| Canvas White | `#FFFFFF` | `--color-canvas-white` | Page bg, button fills |

**Just 2 colors. Period.**

## Typography (single font, single weight, 2 sizes)
### nb_international_proregular
- Sub: Inter. **Weight 400 only**. **Sizes 11, 16px only**
- Letter-spacing: 0.165px @ 11px, 0.304px @ 16px
- Line-height: 1.05, 1.24, 1.50

### Type Scale
| Role | Size | Tracking |
|------|------|----------|
| body | 16 | 0.304px |
| (footer) | 11 | 0.165px |

## Spacing & Shapes
- **Density: compact**
- Scale: **4, 7, 10, 14, 16, 40 (only 6 values)**
- Card padding: 10-16px
- Element gap: 2-14px

### Border Radius
**6px on EVERYTHING** (single uniform value)

## Components

### Navigation Link
Black text 16px, 0.304px letter-spacing, 1.05 line-height, no bg.

### Button Default
**Transparent bg, 1px black border, 6px radius, padding 40/10/7/10px** (asymmetric!).

### Footer Link
Black text 11px, 0.165px letter-spacing.

## Imagery
**Layered slightly transparent photo-realistic images, often muted/desaturated, overlapping and receding.** Abstract forms, x-rays, natural phenomena. Sharp unrounded rectangles. **Diagonal arrangement creates dynamic depth.**

## Layout
**Full-bleed**, no max-width. **Diagonal arrangement of overlapping image panels** = dynamic depth signature. Fixed-width nav top-left, centered. Continuous flow, no dividers. Linear/direct info architecture.

## Motion Notes
- Image layer crossfades on hover/scroll
- Diagonal overlap may shift on scroll
- Subtle interactive states (color/border)
- Architectural blueprint feel — precise, restrained

## Do's
- **Strictly achromatic** (#FFF + #000 only)
- **6px border-radius on ALL interactive** containers
- nb_international_proregular weight 400 ONLY
- Letter-spacing: 0.165px @ 11px, 0.304px @ 16px
- **Transparent bg + 1px black border** for default buttons
- Compact spacing (2/4/7/10/14/16/40px values)

## Don'ts
- **No chromatic colors AT ALL**
- No drop shadows or elevation
- No font weights other than 400
- **No border-radius other than 6px**
- No decorative gradients/textures
- No large content gaps (density is the design)

## Similar Brands
Büro Uebele, Certain Measures, FutureDeluxe

## Key Insight
**The "ULTIMATE minimal" archetype.** Just 2 colors. ONE font. ONE weight. TWO sizes. SIX-px radius EVERYWHERE. Compact spacing rhythm. The whole design is compact + precise + binary. Diagonal overlapping image layers create the visual interest — typography stays in its lane. Asymmetric button padding (40/10/7/10) is unusual and architectural.
