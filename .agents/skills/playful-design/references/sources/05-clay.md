Source: https://styles.refero.design/style/b5ca4e9a-2322-4796-b4c5-3b3bf194821f
Original URL: https://clay.com
Category scan: Playful Design
Theme: light
North star: Playful Precision Playground.

## Essence

Clay shows playful SaaS at a mature level. The base is clean and professional:
white surfaces, black text, Roobert typography, careful borders, and contained
layouts. Playfulness appears through colorful testimonial cards, accent blocks,
rounded pills, and friendly spacing. It is a strong model for B2B products that
need energy without losing authority.

## Typography

- Roobert for all text.
- Phosphor / Phosphor-Bold for functional/icon elements.
- Display: `60px`, line-height `1`, with `-0.04em`.
- Body: `16px`, line-height `1.6`.
- Caption can go to `10px` with strong tracking.

## Layout

- Centered max width around `1200px`.
- Hero uses large centered headline over subtle texture.
- Alternating text/image sections.
- Multi-column feature and testimonial cards.
- Section gap around `64px`.

## Components

- Primary CTA: black fill, white text, `12px` radius.
- Secondary outline/text button: minimal.
- Ghost button: white fill, black text, `12px` radius.
- Pill button: white fill, black text, huge pill radius.
- Large colorful cards: `40px` radius.
- Hero section card: transparent, `12px` radius, no shadow.

## Reusable Lessons

- Playful SaaS can use black/white as the base and color in content modules.
- Large colorful cards feel mature when type and spacing are disciplined.
- Roobert-style typography creates friendly precision.
- Warm border colors can soften clean interfaces.

## Anti-Patterns

- Do not use sharp corners.
- Do not add new shadows.
- Do not make saturated colors into large text blocks.
- Do not deviate from the set type scale.
