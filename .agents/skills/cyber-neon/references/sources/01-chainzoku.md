## Imagery
**Highly stylized illustrations** + concept art (characters + urban environments). Full-bleed or large contained. Masked/irregular edges. Dimensional but not photorealistic. **Vivid neon colors against dark nocturnal backdrops.** Solid filled icons for game-like feel.

## Layout
Full-bleed atmospheric illustrations. Hero + section bgs. Top bar + sticky LEFT sidebar for section nav. Centered headlines or visually separated blocks with **cut-out container shapes** (angular, dynamic). Mix of 2-column + immersive single-column visuals. Comfortable density, ample breathing room.

## Motion Notes
- Atmospheric illustrations may have subtle parallax
- Gradient sky background slow drift
- Pill CTA hover: lime intensity glow
- Cut-out container shape transitions
- Game-like UI feels alive

## Do's
- Midnight Ink primary text on light bg, Ghost White on dark bg
- **Electric Lime (#cdfb52) for active states + CTAs** ("Play", "Mint")
- Druk Heavy for headlines 100px+ at 0.8 line-height, -0.02em
- Ghost nav links (no bg/padding, lightweight)
- 8px element gap
- 10px cards, 15px navigation radius
- 1px borders for separation (no shadows)

## Don'ts
- No soft shadows or complex elevation
- No generic system fonts for branding (Druk Heavy critical)
- No interface saturation with multiple vibrants (lime + pink for accents only)
- No gradients except Gradient Sky atmospheric
- No large internal padding on nav/buttons
- No arbitrary radii (10/15 only)

## Similar Brands
Cyberpunk 2077, Akira, Arcane (Netflix)

## Key Insight
**Cyber Neon = stark base + radioactive accent.** The base is high-contrast monochrome (#000 + #fffff7 warm white). The Electric Lime `#cdfb52` is the radioactive signature — paired with Cyber Pink as decorative counter-energy. **301px Druk Heavy display** is the typographic flex (almost monumental). The "warm" off-white `#fffff7` instead of pure white is unique — most cyber sites go cold, Chainzoku slightly warm.
