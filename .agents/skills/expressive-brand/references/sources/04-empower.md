# Empower - Expressive Brand Source Notes

## Core read

Empower is the "midnight finance with bright yellow button" archetype: a
high-contrast dark financial brand, heavy condensed/expanded typography, a
single optimistic yellow action color, and a switch into clean white content
sections. The expression is direct and no-nonsense rather than decorative.

This source is useful for brands that need to feel bold, useful, and
memorable, especially in categories where trust usually leads to blandness.

## Typography

- Body/nav: GTAmericaExtended-like font.
- Buttons/nav emphasis: GTAmericaExpanded-like font.
- Display: Gravity-like font, weight 900 at 96px.
- Display can also go extremely large for brand moments.
- Tracking is tight on large and mid-size text.
- Caption/button labels can use wide positive tracking.

## Structure

- Base unit: 8px.
- Density: comfortable.
- Section gap: around 32px in compact modules, larger in hero.
- Card padding: 24px.
- Element gap: 8px.
- Modules: 16px radius.
- Cards: minimum 24px radius.
- Primary buttons: pill-like.

## Expressive lessons

1. One bright action color
   - Yellow is the only saturated accent.
   - It must own primary interaction.

2. Typography is the brand
   - Condensed/expanded forms create voice.
   - Do not replace with generic system font.

3. Dark/light contrast clarifies content
   - Dark hero creates identity.
   - White sections carry information.

4. Shapes stay friendly
   - Large radii soften the bold finance tone.
   - Avoid heavy shadows.

5. Directness is expressive
   - The brand does not need mascots or gradients.
   - High contrast plus strong type is enough.

## Anti-patterns

- Adding extra saturated colors.
- Heavy shadows.
- Generic fonts for headlines.
- Low-contrast yellow text.
- Too many decorative graphics.
- Rounded-card SaaS sameness without typographic force.
